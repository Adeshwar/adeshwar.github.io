Ceremony Flow

Baarat ArrivalThe groom arrives with joyous music, dancing, and celebration as his family and friends accompany him to the venue in a lively procession.
Groom EntryThe groom makes his ceremonial entrance, welcomed by the bride’s family in a gesture of love and unity.
Bride EntryThe bride enters gracefully, surrounded by her bridesmaids or family, symbolizing purity, love, and anticipation for the union.
Mala ExchangeThe couple exchanges floral garlands (malas) as a sign of acceptance and mutual respect, marking the beginning of their sacred bond.
Ganesh PujaSeeking the blessings of Lord Ganesha, the remover of obstacles, to ensure an auspicious start to the ceremony and a harmonious married life.
KanyadaanThe bride’s parents lovingly entrust their daughter to the groom, symbolizing the merging of two families and the parents’ blessings for the couple’s future.
HawanThe sacred fire ritual, where offerings are made to Agni (the Fire God), representing the divine witness to the marriage vows.
Mangal PherasThe couple circles the holy fire seven times, reaffirming their promises to support and cherish each other throughout their lives.
SaptpadiThe seven symbolic steps taken together by the couple, each representing a vow for love, trust, prosperity, and lifelong companionship.
Thali & SindoorThe groom adorns the bride with traditional symbols of marriage, marking the sanctity and eternal commitment of their union.
AshirwaadThe couple seeks blessings from elders and loved ones, concluding the ceremony with prayers for a blissful married life.
BidaaiA heartfelt farewell as the bride departs with her husband, symbolizing the beginning of their new life together.
